AISERS Organizational Chart

AISERS Mission

To provide empowerment, assistance, and development to students that will mold them into responsible and well-rounded individuals who aspire to attain their full potential locally and globally. Showcase the capabilities of the department performed inside and outside of the campus premises through academic and non-academic activities.

AISERS Vision

To foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among students of Bachelor of Science in Aviation Information System in order to build a community within the department. To promote mental health awareness and assist students in discovering, developing, and cultivating their passion; whereas to support the department in producing a fully equipped and globally competitive professionals who will soon take part in the aviation industry and other prospect career paths.
